The Photographer

Shabna
Mehabooba

Ontario, Canada

Based in Ontario, Canada, Shabna spends her days chasing light through forests, wetlands, and open skies. With four years behind the lens, her work is rooted in patience — the kind that teaches you to sit still long enough for an owl to turn its gaze, or for the clouds to part at exactly the right moment over a mountain lake.

Every photograph is an invitation to slow down and notice what has always been there.
